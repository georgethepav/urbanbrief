Process

Step by step, from idea to application.

Whether it’s a rear extension or apartments in a former office building, UrbanBrief follows a simple, repeatable process designed to give you clarity early.

1
Submit the property

Tell us what’s there now, where it is, and what you’re hoping to do. Attach any agent details, plans or photos you have.

2
We triage & propose a route

We review the basics and suggest the right first step – most often a £135 Planning Reality Check, or a more detailed Formal Planning Review.

3
We review policy & guidance

For Reality Checks, we carry out a desktop study using local policy, mapping and permitted development rules. For deeper work, we also use official UK planning guidance channels where appropriate.

4
You receive clear advice

You get a written summary, a simple likelihood score (for Reality Checks), and our view on whether to proceed to pre-app, full plans or to walk away.

UrbanBrief provides independent professional interpretation of planning policy, guidance and informal advice. Nothing we provide is a guarantee of permission or a substitute for a formal council decision.
