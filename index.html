Process

Step by step, from idea to application.

Whether it’s a rear extension, a bar in a former bank or apartments in a city office building, UrbanBrief follows a simple, repeatable process.

1
Submit the property

Tell us what’s there now, where it is, and what you’re hoping to do. Attach any agent details, plans or photos you have.

2
We triage & propose a route

We review the basics and suggest the right entry service – usually a Planning Reality Check or Formal Planning Review.

3
We use official guidance

For Reality Checks, we use Planning Portal’s guidance services and other approved channels to obtain informal advice.

4
You receive clear advice

You get a written summary, risk grading and our view on next steps, with the option to move into pre-app or full plans.

UrbanBrief provides independent professional interpretation of official planning guidance and services. Advice is informal and non-binding, but built around real conversations and real policy.

Pricing & payments

Transparent fees, flexible ways to pay.

The goal is simple: make professional planning advice accessible, whether you’re a homeowner doing one project or an investor looking at multiple sites.

Credit & debit cards – accept card payments for all UrbanBrief services using providers like Stripe or Square (link buttons to your hosted checkout pages).
Bank transfer – traditional invoice and bank payment options for clients who prefer to pay on invoice.
Digital wallets – support services such as PayPal or similar for quick, secure payment (configure via your chosen provider).

Retainers & bundles – for agents, auction houses and repeat investors, fixed-fee bundles or monthly retainers can be agreed.
